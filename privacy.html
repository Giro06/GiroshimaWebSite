This privacy notice outlines how Giroshima Games ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you access our mobile applications, website, and related services. Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
